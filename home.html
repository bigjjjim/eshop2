Home


One Earth is a small company situated in Australia that tries to promote non-plastic products in the goal of reducing pollution and other nefast effect from using plastic.

On this website you will find a selection of the best products on the market for a suprinzingly cheap price. On top of that, 10% of any sold product is given to charity associations that helps fighting against global warming and deforestation.

Furthermore, in the quiz section of the website, we provide an entertaining way of finding which element of this world suit you the best. Our planet is composed of different elements which, in turn, make us what we are.

One earth aims at reminding humanity how grateful we should be for these elements.
